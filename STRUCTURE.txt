Routes Map
==========

Public Routes:
  /                                  → LandingPage
  /browse                            → BrowseSimulations
  /creator                           → CreatorDashboard
  /creator/build                     → SimulationBuilder

Simulation Routes (dynamic :id):
  /simulation/customer-comments/:id  → CustomerCommentsSimulation
  /simulation/sales-negotiation/:id  → SalesNegotiationSimulation
  /simulation/prioritization/:id     → PrioritizationSimulation
  /simulation/team-conflict/:id      → TeamConflictSimulation


Data Flow
=========

1. localStorage (storage.js)
   ↓
2. Default Simulations + Custom Simulations
   ↓
3. Browse Page (BrowseSimulations.jsx)
   ↓
4. User selects simulation
   ↓
5. Routed to specific simulation component
   ↓
6. User completes simulation
   ↓
7. Score saved to localStorage (saveProgress)
   ↓
8. Results displayed


Creator Flow
============

1. Creator Dashboard (/creator)
   ↓
2. Click "Create New Simulation"
   ↓
3. Simulation Builder (/creator/build)
   ↓
4. Fill form → Generate with AI (mocked)
   ↓
5. Review generated scenario
   ↓
6. Save & Publish
   ↓
7. Saved to localStorage (custom simulations)
   ↓
8. Appears in Browse page
